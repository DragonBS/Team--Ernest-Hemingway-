Welcome to the GAME. Here are the main instructions you
would need to play the game.

To move your hero horizontally or vertically,
please, use the Arrow keys.
To attack an enemy, please, use the Space bar.
To open your inventory, please, press "i".
To open the skills menu, please, press "s".

Press ENTER to return to the Main menu.

Press ESC to exit the game at any time.
